Website Evaluations

Good Website: Apple.com

This is a solid website with a clean layout, excellent contrast, and consistent navigation.

  • Contrast: The site uses clear contrast between the background and text, making it easy to read.
  • Repetition: The same fonts and colors are used throughout the site, keeping the website style consistent
  • Alignment: All elements are aligned, making the site feel organized and professional.
  • Proximity: Related items like menus and links are placed together to show relevance.

Bad Website: Arngren.net

This website has poor layout and readability issues due to bad use of contrast and misaligned elements.

  • Contrast: The text is hard to read because, well, it has no real formatting
  • Repetition: There's inconsistency in the use of fonts and colors, making the site feel disjointed.
  • Alignment: Text and images are misaligned, creating a cluttered look.
  • Proximity: While relevant items are grouped together, it's... well, it's, i dont have words to describe this.
